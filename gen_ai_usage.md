# Generative AI Usage Declaration

This document outlines the use of Generative AI tools during the development of the SkillBridge platform, pitch deck, and accompanying documentation. 

In accordance with academic and professional transparency standards, we declare the following AI usage:

## 1. Prototype Scaffolding & UI Generation
**Tool Used:** Lovable (lovable.dev)

* **Purpose:** We utilised [Lovable](https://lovable.dev/) to accelerate the initial frontend development and build the skeleton prototype of our platform.
* **Extent of Use:** The AI was used to generate boilerplate React components, basic layout structures, and standard UI patterns (such as navigation bars and form scaffolding).
* **Human Contribution:** Once the skeleton was generated, our team took full ownership of the codebase. We heavily customised the components, integrated the backend (Supabase), implemented the complex state management, wrote the custom matching logic, and refined the UI/UX to match our specific branding and business requirements.

## 2. Copywriting, Grammar, and Script Refinement
**Tools Used:** Large Language Models (e.g., ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude)

* **Purpose:** We used conversational AI to assist with spelling, punctuation, and grammatical review across our written materials.
* **Extent of Use:** AI tools were used to proofread the pitch deck content, refine the flow of our presentation scripts, and ensure professional formatting. We also utilised AI to help reformat our script notes into structured tables for our demo walkthrough.
* **Human Contribution:** All core ideas, business models, market research data, user testing results, and feature definitions were entirely generated by our team. The AI acted strictly as a proofreader and formatting assistant, not as a content creator.
